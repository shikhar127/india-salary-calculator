Full Description (≤ 4000 characters)
SalaryFit is the simplest, most complete salary calculator for India — built for FY 2025-26. Whether you're evaluating a job offer, planning a hike negotiation, or figuring out how much CTC you need for a target take-home — SalaryFit does it all, instantly and offline. ────────────────────────────── WHAT YOU CAN DO ────────────────────────────── ▸ Calculate Take-Home Salary Enter your CTC and see your exact monthly in-hand salary. Breaks down Basic, HRA, PF, Professional Tax, and Income Tax under both New and Old regimes. ▸ Compare New vs Old Tax Regime Side-by-side comparison of New Regime and Old Regime — with all major deductions (80C, 80D, HRA exemption, NPS, LTA) accounted for. Know exactly which regime saves you more and by how much. ▸ Hike Projections See your new take-home after any salary hike — instantly. Compare your current and post-hike salary with accurate tax impact. ▸ Reverse Calculator Working backwards: enter the monthly take-home you want, and SalaryFit tells you the CTC you need to negotiate. ────────────────────────────── BUILT FOR INDIA ────────────────────────────── • FY 2025-26 tax slabs (Budget 2025) • Standard deduction ₹75,000 under New Regime • Full Old Regime deduction support: 80C, 80D, HRA, NPS, LTA, and more • State-wise Professional Tax (Maharashtra, Karnataka, WB, and more) • Accurate Employee PF (12% of Basic, configurable) • Configurable Basic Salary % for precise CTC splitting ────────────────────────────── COMPLETELY FREE ────────────────────────────── • No ads — ever • No account or login required • Works fully offline • No data collected — all calculations happen on your device, nothing is sent anywhere Perfect for salaried professionals, HR teams, job seekers, and anyone navigating India's income tax system.

How to Export PNG

Option 1 — macOS Preview (easiest):
Download the SVG → Open in Preview → File → Export → PNG.
Set width/height to exact pixel size required.

Option 2 — Browser screenshot:
Right-click the SVG above → "Save image as" (saves SVG).
Open in Inkscape (free) → Export PNG at exact size.

Option 3 — Figma:
Copy the SVG source, paste into Figma, export as PNG at 1×.

Play Store requirements:
· Icon: 512 × 512 px PNG, max 1 MB, no alpha required
· Feature graphic: 1024 × 500 px PNG, max 1 MB
· Screenshots: min 2, phone 9:16 ratio recommended
