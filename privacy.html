Privacy Policy

SalaryFit  ·  Last updated: February 24, 2026

SalaryFit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations happen entirely on your device.

1. About This App

SalaryFit is an India salary calculator for FY 2025-26. It helps you calculate take-home salary, compare tax regimes, project hike scenarios, and reverse-calculate required CTC — entirely offline, with no account required.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect your salary, CTC, income, or any financial figures you enter.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or any third-party tracking SDKs.

3. Local Storage

The app may save your Annual CTC to your device's local storage (browser localStorage / app sandbox) so it can be pre-filled on your next visit. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is never transmitted to any server.
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser/app data.

4. Internet Access

The Android app requests internet permission solely to load the web interface from the app bundle. No outbound network requests are made to any server by the app itself. No data is sent anywhere.

5. Third-Party Services

SalaryFit does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or payment processors.

6. Children's Privacy

SalaryFit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely to affect users in any meaningful way.
